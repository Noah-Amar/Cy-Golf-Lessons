About Cat Silva

Cypress resident and Professional Golf Instructor Cat Silva has been providing local golf instruction for over twenty years. His commitment to offering well planned, instructive golf lessons at affordable prices has stood the test of time.

Cat teaches a game within everyone's reach. He has taught married couples, seniors, teenagers, weekend golfers and even complete beginners. He wants all of his students to have fun while improving their skill level on the course.

Golf for All Levels

Cy Golf Instruction caters to all players and ages, whether you’re a beginner or a pro. Lessons can include putting, hitting the ball off the tee, chipping, sand game, full swing biomechanics and strategy sessions.
