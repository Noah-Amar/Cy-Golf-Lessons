About Me

I Believe Golf is for Everyone.

My mission is to encourage, educate and inspire anyone of any age, gender or level to pick up a set of golf clubs and achieve success.

Cat's Legacy

Cypress resident and Professional Golf Instructor Cat Silva has been providing local golf instruction for over twenty years. His commitment to offering well planned, instructive golf lessons at affordable prices has stood the test of time. Cat teaches a game within everyone's reach. He has taught married couples, seniors, teenagers, weekend golfers and even complete beginners. He wants all of his students to have fun while improving their skill level on the golf course.
